## Overview

The **Content Writer** is a background agent that turns the work your team already does — resolving support tickets, merging product changes, answering questions in Slack — into drafted documentation, routed to the right surface: a **developer-docs pull request** or a **draft Help Center article**.

Your Content Writer comes with three sub-agents on its Visual Builder canvas — referenced by name throughout these guides:

- **Router Agent** — decides whether each draft becomes a docs PR or a Help Center article
- **KB Article Agent** — writes the Help Center articles (Zendesk)
- **Page Drafter** — writes the Notion drafts

## What you get

- **Capture novel resolutions from support** — when a rep solves something that isn't in your docs yet, it becomes a drafted update instead of disappearing when the ticket closes.
- **Keep docs in sync with the product** — code changes and releases become drafted doc updates, so docs stop trailing the product.
- **Lift your AI-channel visibility** — more accurate, more complete content is what LLMs and AI search cite back to your users.
